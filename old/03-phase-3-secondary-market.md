# Phase 3: Secondary Market Module

## Apartments for Sale, Rent & Daily Rental

**Timeline:** Weeks 12-18  
**Dependency:** Phase 1 & 2 Complete

---

## 3.1 Overview

The Secondary Market module handles:
- Apartments for Sale (from owners/agents)
- Apartments for Rent (long-term)
- Daily Rental (short-term/Airbnb style)
- Commercial Properties
- Land Plots
- Houses (individual, not projects)
